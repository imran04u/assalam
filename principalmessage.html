Principal’s Message

I am privileged to be part of an educational setting which is enriching, encouraging and rewarding for the whole community.

Welcome to AS-SALAM ISLAMIC COLLEGE, a student of this college will become an A’alim and at the same time, he will be a graduate after the completion of 5 years course (Affiliated to Bharathidasan University, Trichirappalli – 620 023) with the exciting vision to educate and prepare its students as morally committed professionals, intellectuals, community leaders Social Analyzers and Murabees who are grounded in the Islamic scholarly tradition and are conversant with the cultural currents and critical ideas in shaping the modern society.

I am privileged to be part of an educational setting which is enriching, encouraging and rewarding for the whole community.

A valued and significant opportunity for every student is that of the delivery of the Arabic language, as well as studies in Islam, Arabic and the Holy Quran, to enhance the moral, spiritual, physical and intellectual abilities of our students. Central to our values are Pride, Respect, Responsibility, Resilience and Inclusion.

Today’s world is governed by science, technology and corporate; the leftist ideologies and the writings of the third world are gaining popularity. In this arena, we need knowledgeable and talented Islamic scholars to tackle the challenges of the above factors. Our college aims at producing the graduates with versatile talents and leadership qualities. In turn, these graduates will face the challenges of the modern world and lead the mankind towards the right path; they will contribute in all the fields of education. Our syllabus is designed in such a way to achieve the above said objectives.
