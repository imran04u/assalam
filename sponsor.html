Dear Brother, Sisters in Islam

May Almighty Allah’s peace be upon all of us.

By the grace of Almighty Allah we have achieved the long term of establishing an Arabic College at Trichy. Inspite of various financial hurdles, the college is functioning with the aim of producing the A’alims who will lead the society in all walks of life.

We request your fruitful contribution by sponsoring one student expenses and we pray to Allah that your sacrifice will be a Sadaqathul Jariyah and you will be get the reward for the noble deeds done by your sponsored student.

May accept and reward our pure intention and noble deeds.
