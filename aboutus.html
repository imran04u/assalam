As-Salam Islamic College

A deep Understanding in basic Islamic disciplines such as Al-Quraan, As-Sunnah, Fiqh, Aqeedha, Thareekh and Usools etc.. and to develop the skill in applying this knowledge wherever required.

VISION

"As-Salam Islamic college aims to educate and prepare its students as morally committed professionals, intellectuals, community leaders, Social Analyzers and Murabees who are grounded in the Islamic scholarly tradition and are conversant with the cultural currents and critical ideas in shaping the modern society"

MISSION

  • To provide education to students and facilitate them to play their appropriate role effectively in the contemporary world.
  • To train and enable the students to establish Islam in all walks of life and contribute to the cause of Islam and humanity in general.
  • To provide abundant resources and create a learning environment to the students.
  • To give proper training to students in using the media to disseminate knowledge.

GOALS AND OBJECTIVES

  • A deep Understanding in basic Islamic disciplines such as Al-Quraan, As-Sunnah, Fiqh, Aqeedha, Thareekh and Usools etc.. and to develop the skill in applying this knowledge wherever required.
  • Ability to read and comprehend classical and modern works in Arabic and English languages, to engage in conversations, to deliver prepared speeches and to write simple essays, administrative documents and also to ensure clear understanding of Arabic grammar rules.
  • Ability to express ideas orally and verbally in Tamil language and a working knowledge in other languages.
  • Leadership & management skills such as planning, organizing, implementing, communicating and office administration.
  • Understanding of modern concepts and practices in Politics, Economics, Sociology etc. and a deep awareness of current international and local trends, especially with respect to India and the Muslim community.
  • A basic awareness about other major world religions and contemporary ideologies.
  • Basic life skills and skills in research methodology, psychology, and IT etc.
  • Understanding of the contemporary Islamic movements, their thoughts and methodologies and experience in Da’wa activities.
  • Spiritual qualities such as deep faith, eagerness in ibada, noble characters, piousness in life and desire to live for Islam
